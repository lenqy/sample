About Me

I am Ma. Elena Fuentes, and I am from Barili, Cebu, but currently residing at Nasipit, Talamban Cebu. Currently taking up, Certificate in Computer Technology in University of San Carlos - Talamban Campus. My interests before was teaching and volunteering, but as time went by, I found myself interested in coding and web development as well, making these two disciplines the perfect choice for me. I am a bit of a bookworm, especially when I have nothing to do. I love curling up with a good mystery when I am at home! You can see that I don't spend a lot of time outside. I adore rainy days because I can cuddle up with a blanket and eat lots of foods which really gives me satisfaction! I also love coding, doing some drafting and solving mysteries, you know why? It is because I feel like I have a superpower because of it! Nevertheless, I am one of the scholars of Passerelles Numeriques Philippines. Lastly, if I had to describe myself in one word, it would be dynamic as I accept challenges with a positive spirit, and I am always eager to learn.
